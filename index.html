About Urban Splendour School

Our History

Urban Splendour Nursery and Primary School was founded in 2014 by a visionary educator with a distinguished background in education, having served as Director of Education in the Lagos State Ministry of Education. The school commenced operations on September 16, 2015, with a modest enrollment of 80 students and a team of 15 dedicated staff members.

Over the years, Urban Splendour has evolved into a thriving institution,with modern facilities such as 18 standard Classrooms,Art, ICT, and Music Rooms, a Sick Bay, and a playground equipped with state-of-the-art play gadgets. These resources enable us to deliver a comprehensive education that nurtures academic excellence, creativity, and personal growth.
